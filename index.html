About Us

SPΔRX Holdings (Pvt) Ltd. brings over a decade of custom office and house construction and remodeling experience in the Sri Lanka Our team of experienced Engineers, Architects and Interior Designers can handle your toughest criteria and requirements with efficiency and distinction.

We are ready to work with your ideas, or develop a plan with you that will suit your style and fit your budget. We take pride in the quality of our work, we want you to be satisfied and we delight in delivering beyond your expectations. Our work encompasses everything from the smallest renovations to beautiful custom house designs, house construction and office spaces. Our experience brings to clients first-class workmanship with fast track capabilities from design to house construction completion.

Our Vision

To be the best in the Construction field in the world, new concepts in designs and improve new technologies to get good quality and cut down unnecessary expenses.



Our Mission

Leader of innovative concepts and designs to radically change the construction industry in the universe. To provide high quality products and services to customers through integrity and best practices.
